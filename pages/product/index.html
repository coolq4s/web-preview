Our Product

Black Pepper
Actually black pepper and white pepper come from the same tree. However, the different processes make them both look and taste different.
White Pepper
White pepper is taken from ripe peppers. The next process is to soak the pepper in salt water so that the skin peels off and leaves only the inside.
Ginger
Basically, the three types of ginger contain the same, namely carbohydrates, fiber, protein, minerals such as iron and potassium, and vitamins such as vitamin C.
Red Ginger
Red ginger has several ingredients in it. Including Phenolic, Essential Oils, Zingeron, Gingerol. This spice is very good for the human body.
Turmeric
Turmeric contains curcumin which works to increase endurance. That is, the body's defense system against viruses or bacteria that cause disease is improved, not directly killing these germs.
Galangal
Galangal contains about 1% greenish yellow volatile oil which mainly consists of 48% methyl cinnamate, 20%-30% cineol, eugenol, 1% camphor, sesquiterpenes.
Clove
Every 2 grams of clove contains 21 calories of energy, 1 gram of carbohydrates, 1 gram of fiber. Also able to meet 30% of food needs, 4% vitamin K, and 3% vitamin C of the body's daily needs.
Cinnamon
Cinnamon contains protein, fiber, calcium, iron, potassium, selenium, phosphorus, vitamin A, vitamin B, vitamin K, as well as substances that have antioxidant, antibacterial, and anti-inflammatory effects.
Briquettes
We have 3 types of briquettes including for baking, regular, and premium (usually used for shisha cigarettes).
